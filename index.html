Our team created an online experience for a Lisbon-based startup called Eat & Travel. Eat & Travel restaurants will create immersive experiences that convey the sense of travel through exotic foods and virtual boat tours.

OUR PROTOTYPE: Eat & Travel Online

The challenge for this project was to create an interactive solution that would benefit patrons of Eat & Travel restaurants. Our final design needed to be based on a deep understanding of the problem space, as well as usability testing, to ensure that our final working prototype could be immediately adapted to real-world use.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Our project was part of the capstone project requirement for the 16-month double masters of human-computer interaction from Carnegie-Mellon University, USA and the University of Madeira, Portugal.

Over the duration of the project our multidisciplinary team worked with our project sponsor, Eat & Travel, to create an online experience to communicate their startup to potential franchise clients and restaurant customers.

RESEARCH PROCESS

During the spring semester, our team conducted in-depth, user-centered research in order to gain understanding of the project area. The goal of this semester was to identify key insights from the research findings, and develop several concepts for a final product.

During the fall semester, we worked with our client to select, refine, test, and implement a final working solution.

PROJECT STATEMENT

We want to bring travel to the dining experience.
